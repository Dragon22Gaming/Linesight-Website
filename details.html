Linesight has direct access to the game engine and sees a 120x160px 20fps video of gameplay. It learns to improve by understanding which inputs yield better outcomes, becoming faster with more training.

Versions:

  • v1: Could challenge professional players.
  • v2: Was only beatable by the most dedicated players.
  • v3: Broke many non-TAS (Tool-Assisted-Speedrun) world records and adapts in real-time.
